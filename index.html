What is Momentz?

Momentz is a European growth and venture architecture firm based in Milan, Italy. Founded to bridge the gap between strategy and execution, Momentz helps local companies expand internationally and global companies enter new markets. The firm operates as a hybrid operator model — combining strategy, execution, and selective investment under one roof. Unlike traditional consulting firms that only advise, or VCs that only fund, Momentz builds from within: structuring operations, scaling revenue, and co-creating ventures alongside founders.

What does Momentz do?

Momentz operates across three core pillars: (1) Expansion Architecture — structured market entry including go-to-market design, local partnerships, and revenue acceleration; (2) Growth Execution — operational structuring, commercial scaling, and performance optimization; (3) Venture Catalyst — co-building ventures from ideation to market, including business model structuring, fundraising strategy, and selective capital allocation.

Frequently asked.

A growth and venture architecture firm combines strategic advisory, operational execution, and selective capital allocation into one model. Rather than just advising (like consultants) or just funding (like VCs), Momentz builds alongside the companies it works with — structuring market entry, scaling operations, and co-creating ventures from the ground up.

Momentz partners with mid-size companies ready to scale internationally, scale-ups entering new markets, and founders building category-defining startups. On the investment side, Momentz also works with investors seeking structured venture creation and corporates looking for new growth engines. The firm is selective by design and partners with a limited number of companies at any given time.

Momentz is headquartered in Milan, Italy, with an operating geography that spans Europe and beyond. The firm helps local European companies expand internationally and assists global companies in entering and scaling within new markets across the continent.

Traditional consulting firms deliver strategy decks and recommendations. Momentz goes further: it executes the strategy, structures the operations, and in many cases co-invests alongside the companies it supports. Every engagement is built for long-term enterprise value, not billable hours. Momentz builds from within, not advises from the outside.

Momentz's portfolio includes Korus, an AI-powered content platform bringing media advertising precision to the creator economy; Renovation Studio, a premium renovation management service; and Lens.Design, an AI-driven platform turning visual inspiration into actionable discovery for architecture and design. Additional ventures are continuously in development.
